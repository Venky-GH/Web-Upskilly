Emotional Quotient


Scenario based learning modules, which focus on why, what and how each and every minute aspect of a salesperson’s behavior impact the customer’s buying behavior.

We align your Frontline employees to the business goals and ensure that they are up for the mission! Key topics covered:

  • Dress code
  • Language
  • Mannerisms
  • Etiquette
  • Rapport building
  • Trust building
  • Handling angry/Rude customers
  • Handling bad products & returns
  • Types of customers & how to handle them
  • Billing issues

Intelligence Quotient


Sales personnel in a B2C have very less time to interact and convince a customer to buy a product. They need to be quick on their feet, ask the right questions and sometimes deal with different types of customers at the same time all in quick time. We help your sales team do just that.

  • Uncovering needs
  • Motivating buying behavior
  • Objection handling
  • Closing sales
  • Adding items to basket
  • Follow up & referrals
  • Thought-building exercises
