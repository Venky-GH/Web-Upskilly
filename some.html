Meet

We will be the extended L&D branch of your company not just a company you have outsourced content development to. That means, we have your best interests in mind. We don’t go about transforming each and every piece of content.

Our process starts with a visit from our Learning doctors:

a. Organizational perspective: They collaborate with business heads to understand the mission & targets of the organization and diagnose the skill gaps.

b. Employee perspective: Employees are the key to our process. The learning doctors conduct surveys, focussed Group Discussions & interviews to:

Understand challenges with the current learning systems
Understand the mode of learning effective for them
Diagnose the learning culture
